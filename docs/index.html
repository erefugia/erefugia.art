Rewilding Hypergraphia

By Laughing Lion Silly Monkie

Embracing the paradoxes of the human experience, Rewilding Hypergraphia invites us to witness the author's journey of healing and transformation while also exploring what it means to live in harmony with ourselves, each other, and the natural world. Across fifty poems, prayers, short works of prose, and spoken word pieces full of vivid imagery and unexpected encounters—such as a conversation with a talking bumblebee—the author reveals the beauty and messiness of life, offering glimpses of both profound insight and laugh-out-loud humor. The work is a call to reclaim our authenticity and creative potential, find solace in the natural world, reconnect with one another, and embrace the transformative power of simply being present in the moment.

With original paintings, drawings, and mandalas bridging visual and verbal worlds, Rewilding Hypergraphia is a gentle tree, growing from seed to star, reminding us of the sacred in the simplest things. It is a portal between hearts and a bridge between hands, reassuring us that we are not alone. And it is a two-thousand-pound bomb of raw compassion and passion, challenging all systems of suppression, repression, and oppression that hold us back from living the life we yearn for. The book invites readers to question, awaken, and embrace the wild, unstoppable creative force within themselves, find the courage to uplift the world and one another, speak truth to power, and discover simple joys, even through life’s darkest nights.

Breathe. Release. Co-create.

The Work Within

In this section, you’ll find a brief glimpse into each of the 50 chapters—each one a unique exploration of themes like gratitude, inner transformation, love, and humanity’s interconnectedness. Whether you’re drawn to reflective prose, playful poems, or vibrant art, these summaries offer a taste of the experiences and reflections within.
